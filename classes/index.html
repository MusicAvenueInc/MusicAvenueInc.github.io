Color Splash After Party - July 31

Music Avenue is offering a class in collaboration with Explore Children's Museum for their Color Splash After Party in the parking lot of the museum on July 31st at 5:30 PM. This music class is free and open to anyone to join.

This class is designed for infants, toddlers, and young children to interact musically with their caregivers. Participants will experience the beauty of artistic movement, steady beat, singing, and listening. Activities are highly interactive, extremely fun, and always free!

  • What: Music Avenue class for children and their caregivers. Pre-registration is requested but walk-ins are always welcome and classes are always free.
  • Who: Caregivers with children! This class is geared for ages 0-5, but all ages, including older siblings, are welcome and encouraged to attend!
  • When: 5:30 - 6:00 PM, Tuesday, July 31
  • Where: Explore Children's Museum Parking Lot, 1433 W Main St, Sun Prairie, WI
  • What to Bring: A blanket to sit on. This class will be held outdoors, so dress accordingly!

*No entrance fee or membership required.

Additional information:
  • The After Party also includes tie dying, which is free for Color Splash Party participants (remember to bring your Color Splash t-shirt!).
  • Extra Color Splash t-shirts will be available for purchase at the After Party while supplies last. Must be a Color Splash t-shirt to tie dye.
  • Pizza and Frios Gourmet Pops will be available for purchase
