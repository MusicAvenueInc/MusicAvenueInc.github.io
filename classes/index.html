Fall 2023 Session

 

Music Avenue is excited to announce our Fall Mini Series!! This set of two classes is designed for infants, toddlers, and young children to interact musically with their caregivers. Participants will experience the beauty of artistic movement, steady beat, singing, and listening. Activities are highly interactive and extremely fun!

Classes are offered in partnership with the Sun Prairie Public Library and will be 30 minutes long and held on the library lawn where there is plenty of space for all.


Register

When: Two Saturdays at 10 AM (you are welcome to attend as many classes in the set as you would like)

  • September 23
  • October 7

Where: Outside on the library lawn between the children's porch and the library sculpture.

Who: These classes are geared for children ages 0-5 and their caregiver(s), but all ages, including older siblings, are welcome and encouraged to attend!

Bring: A blanket or something to sit on

Registration is appreciated, but walk-ins are always welcome!

If the weather is uncooperative, classes will be canceled or moved to the library’s community room. Announcements will be posted here and on the library’s event calendar.

If you want to find out more or stay connected, please contact us.
