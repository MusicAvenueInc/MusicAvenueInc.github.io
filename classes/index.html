Music Avenue at Sun Prairie Public Library

Music Avenue is excited to offer a series of family music classes at the Sun Prairie Public Library amphitheater!

  • What: Music Avenue class for children and their caregivers. Pre-registration is requested but walk-ins are always welcome and classes are always free.
  • Who: Caregivers with children! This class is geared for ages 0-5, but all ages, including older siblings, are welcome and encouraged to attend!
  • When:
    • 3:30 PM - 4:00 PM Sunday, June 21, 2026
    • 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM Saturday, July 11, 2026
    • 10:00 AM - 10:30 AM Saturday, August 1, 2026
  • Where: Sun Prairie Public Library (1350 Linnerud Dr, Sun Prairie, WI 53590), Amphitheater (or the Youth Program Room depending on the weather)
  • *Photographs: Participants may be photographed. Those who do not want their photograph taken should notify Music Avenue staff when they arrive at the class.
