Our Mission

 

Music Avenue, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization whose mission is to provide families in Dane County, Wisconsin with equitable access to early childhood music learning experiences.

 

Our Vision

 

Our vision is to eliminate barriers that prevent families from accessing early childhood music education by providing free music learning experiences to families in Dane County.

 

Our Services

 

We offer free family music classes that focus on infants, toddlers, and young children interacting in music classes with their caregivers.
